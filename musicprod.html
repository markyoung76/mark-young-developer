Music Production

So, you've perhaps already gathered from the previous page about how much I love anything creative? Well, music production and generally all things music related are definitely no exception. Who doesn't find music to be important within their lives? After all, it is the one true universal language that we can all fully appreciate and understand!

It was around 2007, in particular, that I found a different way of accessing my deep-seated love for music. This was done by embarking on a music degree course at Derby University, UK, as a mature student. It provided me with a plethora of performance, production and technology skills and after completing my university studies, I proudly graduated with a 2:1 BA (Hons) Popular Music with Music Technology degree.

I met some great people along the way and had the invaluable experience of essentially becoming a student again, for the first time in over 12 years away from academics (since my teens). In fact, having to adapt to remember how to learn all over again during those few years, has given me the inner-belief that I can go on to use the transferable skills I have acquired during my life to potentially retrain and achieve a change of career. Now, I'm very firmly set towards becoming the best software developer I can be; through continuous hard work, self-directed study (home coding) and sheer determination.
